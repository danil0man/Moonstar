How To Purchase MoonStar

  1. Download MetaMask or Trust Wallet Applications
  2. Purchase Binance Tokens (BNB) and send them to your MetaMask or Trust Wallet.
  3. On the home page of MoonStarToken.com click on “Purchase Tokens” button. This will open a Dapp Exchange Called “PancakeSwap”.
  4. A popup will open, click the check box “I understand”, this will load MoonStar into PancakeSwap.
  5. Make sure your MetaMask Wallet or Trust Wallet Is synced with PancakeSwap.
  6. Set the amount of Moonstar you would like to purchase and press Swap!
  7. Lastly, enjoy your trip to the moon!
